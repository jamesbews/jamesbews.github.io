I have worked on several Freelance web design projects for various clients in the past, most of which are basic business websites. I have worked with several Content Management Systems (CMS) or website builders, however, I have the most experience with WordPress.

The majority of web design work I have completed for clients is building a basic business website. These usually consist of a landing page, list of services, about and contact section. Online Marketplace

I have also created more complex web design projects. In one particular case, a client needed a website to sell digital products online. I implemented WooCommerce on the WordPress website, which allowed the client to manage and sell products as well as make sales and take payments.
